Michael Jordan Career Bests: Points

Michael Jordan scores a career high 51 points (2001)

On December 12, 2001, Michael Jordan set his career high in points in a NBA game. That day he scored 51 points in Washington Wizards's home win against New Orleans Hornets, 107-90. He also had 7 rebounds, 4 assists, 3 steals. He shot 21/36 from two, 0/2 from three, shooting at 55.3% from the field. He also shot 9/10 from the free-throw line.

Michael Jordan Career Bests: Rebounds

Michael Jordan grabs a career high 14 rebounds (2003)
